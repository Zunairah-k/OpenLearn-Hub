1. Introduction

Welcome to OpenLearn Hub! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the necessary information to enhance your experience on OpenLearn Hub:

  • Account Data: When you sign up, we store your email and password securely.
  • Feedback Form Data: If you submit feedback, we may collect your name (optional) and comments to improve our platform.

We do not sell, share, or misuse your personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To improve OpenLearn Hub based on feedback.
  • To respond to inquiries or technical issues.

We do not engage in targeted advertising or share your data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Links

Our platform may include links to external websites. While we aim to recommend useful resources, we do not control their privacy policies. We encourage you to review their terms separately.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect user data, including password encryption and basic security protocols. However, as OpenLearn Hub is a student-led project still in development, we recommend users follow best security practices, such as using strong passwords.

6. Changes to This Policy

As we continue to improve OpenLearn Hub, this Privacy Policy may be updated. Any significant changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, reach out to us at openlearnhub25@gmail.com.
